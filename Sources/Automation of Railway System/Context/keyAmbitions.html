Key Ambitions

To move towards these ambitions, it is now crucial to integrate existing products, technical bricks delivered by R&I programs, in particular Shift2Rail, while facilitating the introduction of further innovations.

This will enable the delivery of new solutions enhancing railway performance:

  • advanced interoperable train control systems;
  • advanced traffic management (TMS);
  • towards Next Generation of Control-Command 4.0;
  • intelligent and autonomous vehicles;
  • automation of the logistics chain.
