Advanced Interoperable Train Control systems

These will deliver more flexibility in operation therefore better real-time adaption to the demand and economic competitiveness by increased capacity.

Key technology enablers:

  • ACS/FRMCS: will lead to more fast, safe, secure, reliable and high capacity connectivity in railway system;
  • Global Navigation Satellite Systems (GNSS), notably Galileo: will lead to more safe, reliable and accurate train positioning.

Main objectives:

  • Advanced train control and monitoring systems, in particular traction, brakes and doors control aiming to better performance;
  • A reliable environment perception through the identification of all external hazards (obstacle detection, environmental monitoring) that is essential to enable on-board decision-making intelligence (before possible collision but also after collision to speed up the recovery time);
  • For urban rail, advanced train control systems will continue to be developed around CBTC solutions and new autonomy capabilities.
