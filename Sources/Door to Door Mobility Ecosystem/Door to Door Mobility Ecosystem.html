Integrate the Railways in a Door to Door Mobility Ecosystem

End-users/citizens’ needs are changing with the introduction of digital technologies. People are always connected and expect mobility offers which are versatile, resilient, selected in one click, with fully transparent information, going more and more towards “on demand” door-to-door integrated mobility. The success of new “modes” of transport are today largely based on their capacity to answer these digital needs.
