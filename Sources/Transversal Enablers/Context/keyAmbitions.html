Key Ambitions

Further investigating digital opportunities can help rail to progress towards key ambition. To move towards achieving these ambitions, it is now crucial to integrate existing products, the technical bricks delivered by R&I programs, in particular Shift2Rail, while facilitating the introduction of further innovations.

This will enable the delivery of new means of enhancing railway performance:

  • new materials and manufacturing techniques;
  • reasonable security measures;
  • cyber security protection;
  • telecommunication;
  • digital technologies (e.g. Big Data, AI, General Intelligence).
